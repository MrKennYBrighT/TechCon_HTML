History

TechCon began in 2018 as a small gathering of local tech enthusiasts with a shared vision for innovation. From humble beginnings in a university lecture hall, it has grown exponentially each year, attracting a global audience of professionals, academics, and students.

Over the years, TechCon has evolved to address the ever-changing landscape of technology. We've introduced new tracks covering emerging fields like AI ethics, quantum computing, and sustainable tech, always striving to stay at the forefront of industry trends.

Our commitment to fostering a community of learning and collaboration remains unwavering. TechCon continues to be a platform where ideas are exchanged, partnerships are forged, and the future of technology is shaped.

Mission

Our mission at TechCon is to:

  • Educate: Provide cutting-edge knowledge and insights from leading experts in various technology domains.
  • Innovate: Inspire new ideas, encourage research, and showcase groundbreaking technological advancements.
  • Connect: Facilitate networking opportunities that foster collaboration, mentorship, and professional growth.
  • Empower: Equip attendees with the skills and vision to drive positive change through technology.

Past Speakers

Dr. Anya Sharma

Dr. Anya Sharma is a renowned AI ethicist and professor of computer science. At TechCon 2022, she delivered a powerful keynote on "AI for Good: Navigating the Ethical Landscape," emphasizing the importance of responsible AI development. Her work has significantly influenced policy discussions around data privacy and algorithmic bias.

Mr. Ben Carter

Mr. Ben Carter, CEO of QuantumLeap Innovations, is a pioneer in quantum computing. His TechCon 2021 talk, "The Quantum Revolution: From Theory to Reality," captivated audiences by demystifying complex quantum concepts and outlining the potential impact on various industries. His company is at the forefront of developing commercial quantum solutions.

Ms. Chloe Davis

Ms. Chloe Davis is a leading cybersecurity expert and founder of SecureNet Solutions. Her TechCon 2023 session, "Fortifying Our Digital Future: Advanced Cyber Threats and Defenses," provided critical insights into emerging cyber threats and practical strategies for robust digital security. She is a strong advocate for public education on online safety.
